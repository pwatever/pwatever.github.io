FOODS ARE YUMMY

Our names are Alison and Paulette

We love food

There are so many different types:

Tabouleh is one of the classic syrian salads. It contains batdones, tomatoes, cucumbers and birchol. It conatains a lemon dressing to make it taste yummy. It is a very delicious salad :)

Yebra is a delicious-lemony, leaf filled with meat and rice. Paulette and Alison eat this every week on the Holy Sabbath, with their families and bond over the delicous taste.

Calsones are chesse filled doughy delicious pastries. They are cooked to perfection with melted butter on top. It is usually served with noodles.

Kibbeh is a delicious food. It is a doughey appetizer that boats eat before the Holy Sabbath meal. It falls under the category of Mazza.
